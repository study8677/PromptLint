# Code of Conduct

We want a welcoming, respectful community.

## Expectations
- Be kind and professional.
- Assume good intent and give constructive feedback.
- Respect different viewpoints and experiences.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment or discrimination.
- Personal attacks or threats.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation for violations.

If you experience issues, please open a private issue or contact the maintainers.
